Privacy Polices

Effective Date: March 1st, 2025

At Earth H20 Digital Architecture Group (Earth H20 DAG), your privacy is important to us. We are dedicated to protecting the personal information you provide while using our website and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we gather, utilize, secure, and share your data when you engage with our digital platforms. By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

  1. Introduction
    This Privacy Policy applies to all individuals and organizations interacting with Earth H20 Digital Architecture Group online. We deliver a broad spectrum of IT services, including consulting, enterprise IT solutions, web development, and digital marketing services. Regardless of your location, we are committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and security of your information while you use our platform.
  1. Information We Collect
    To deliver and improve our services, we collect both personal and non-personal information:
    • Personal Information: This may include your full name, email address, telephone number, company details, and any documents or attachments you voluntarily submit.
    • Non-Personal Information: This consists of technical data such as IP address, browser type, device specifications, and website interaction analytics gathered through cookies and related technologies.
    • Cookies: We utilize cookies to monitor user behavior, evaluate website performance, and enhance overall functionality while personalizing your browsing experience.
    • Optional Messaging: Any voice notes, written messages, or communications submitted through contact forms may be retained to improve our service responsiveness and quality.
  1. Methods of Information Collection
    We obtain your information through the following channels:
    • Voluntary Submission: When you complete inquiry forms, subscribe to newsletters, request services, or communicate with us via email or contact forms.
    • Automated Technologies: Non-personal data is collected automatically through cookies and tracking technologies as you navigate our website.
    • Third-Party Providers: When processing payments or subscribing to services, external payment gateways may collect billing information under their respective privacy policies.
  1. How We Use Your Information
    The data we collect is used for the following purposes:
    • Service Delivery: To respond to inquiries and provide the services you request efficiently.
    • Service Enhancement: To improve website performance, refine user experience, and analyze engagement trends.
    • Marketing Communications: With your approval, we may send newsletters, updates, promotional materials, or industry insights via email or other communication methods.
    • Regulatory Compliance: To fulfill legal obligations, address lawful inquiries, and safeguard our organizational rights.
  1. Data Retention
    We retain personal information only for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including ongoing communication regarding your inquiries or services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly, and we will respond in accordance with applicable regulations.
  1. Payment Security
    To maintain transaction security, Earth H20 Digital Architecture Group works with reliable payment processing partners. All financial data is protected using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption, identifiable by “https://” in your browser. This ensures that sensitive payment information is transmitted securely. Our payment partners comply with PCI-DSS standards to uphold strict data protection measures.
  1. Data Sharing and Disclosure
    We do not sell or lease your personal information. However, information may be shared under the following circumstances:
    • Authorized Service Providers: We may share necessary data with trusted third-party vendors providing services such as hosting, payment processing, analytics, or marketing support. These providers are required to maintain equivalent privacy safeguards.
    • Legal Obligations: Information may be disclosed when required to comply with legal processes, regulatory requirements, or lawful governmental requests.
    • Corporate Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that business transaction.
  1. Data Protection Measures
    We apply industry-recognized security protocols, including encryption technologies, firewalls, and restricted access controls, to protect your personal information. Our security framework is regularly reviewed and updated to align with evolving best practices. While we take reasonable steps to secure your data, no digital system can guarantee complete protection.
  1. Your Rights and Choices
    You maintain certain rights concerning your personal information:
    • Access and Updates: You may request access to the data we hold about you and request corrections where necessary.
    • Data Removal: You may request deletion of your personal information from our records, subject to legal obligations.
    • Marketing Preferences: You can unsubscribe from promotional communications at any time by using the opt-out link in emails or by contacting us directly.
  1. Children's Privacy
    Our services are not designed for individuals under the age of 13. We do not intentionally collect personal data from children. If we become aware that such information has been provided, we will take immediate steps to remove it upon notification.
  1. Confidentiality Commitment
    We treat all personal data with strict confidentiality. Information is securely stored and accessible only to authorized personnel within our organization. Data will not be disclosed to external parties except:
    • With Your Authorization: When you provide explicit consent for specific data sharing.
    • Under Legal Requirement: When disclosure is mandated by court orders, regulatory authorities, or applicable laws.
  1. International Data Transfers
    As a global organization, your personal data may be processed or stored in countries outside your home jurisdiction. We implement appropriate safeguards to ensure that all international data transfers comply with relevant data protection regulations and remain consistent with this Privacy Policy.
  1. Policy Updates
    Earth H20 Digital Architecture Group reserves the right to revise or update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage periodic review to remain informed about how we protect your information.
